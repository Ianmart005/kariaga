Love Cage

for laying eggs

My setup, the Black Soldier Fly love cage is where all the action happens—literally! I designed it to create the perfect environment for the adults to mate and lay eggs. It’s a mesh enclosure, which allows good airflow and plenty of natural light, though I sometimes supplement with UV lights to boost mating activity. Inside, I place attractants like moist cardboard near fermented organic matter where the females lay their eggs. Its been working really well for maintaining a steady breeding cycle, and its key to keeping my BSF production sustainable and efficient. Project study.
